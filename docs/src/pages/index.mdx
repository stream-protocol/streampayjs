## Introduction

Welcome to StreamPay JS, a powerful library that enables seamless integration of StreamPay with Solana Blockchain into your React applications.

## Features

- **Simplified Integration**: StreamPay JS provides easy-to-use React hooks to integrate StreamPay into your application effortlessly.

- **Solana Blockchain Support**: Harness the power of Solana's high-performance blockchain for secure and fast payment processing.

- **Customizable**: Tailor the integration to your specific needs with a range of customization options.
